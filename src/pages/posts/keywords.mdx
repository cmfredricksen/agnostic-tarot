These keywords are just basic meanings, when you are actually doing a reading there will be context which will shape your interpretation of the cards. There is not "one correct" meaning for the cards, except the one you come to associate with any given card after a lot of experience reading.

Whenever you want to you can [Make a Keyword List](/posts/make-a-keyword-list) of your own.
